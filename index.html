Motreebot product proposal Storyboard

The Motreebot Surveillance Service is being used in a mall.

One of the robots is patrolling around the mall’s aisles.

The robot uses AI to identify the faces of people and other situations. Suddenly it identifies that one of the customers is notified in the database as suspicious subject and also identifies that one kid has fallen to the floor.

A notification is sent to the mall’s security and administrative office. The operator watches the live video of the robot.

The monitoring console permits to manage all the fleet of robots, check the reported events and remotely control of the robots.

Go to Monitoring Console Mockup

Other day, the robot is wearing advertising screens while moving close to the customers of the mall. It uses one of its cameras to realize that one of the customers is staring at the screen, hence it stops for a moment and reproduces an audio advertisement related to the content on the screen.

The advertiser receives a detailed report about how effective was the visibility of the purchased advertisement.
